How it works for Buyers

1. Search for posts that meet your interest.

2. Send a message to the owner of the post.

3. Discuss exchange details, including location, time, transaction amount, and transaction means (cash, wire, lightning, etc.).

4. Meet IRL and complete the planned exchange.

How it works for Sellers

1. Create a post in your local area.

2. Relax and wait for Buyers to find your post and express their interest.

3. Discuss exchange details, including location, time, transaction amount, and transaction means (cash, wire, lightning, etc.).

4. Meet IRL and complete the planned exchange.

Some additional suggestions

Here are some additional suggestions for making the exchange process as seamless as possible.

  • Meet in a public location, with plenty of people around.
  • For larger transactions, ask to wait for at least one blockchain confirmation (more if time permits). Here is a decent source for some additional information concerning bitcoin blockchain confirmations.
  • Try to discuss and confirm all of the details concerning the exchange prior to meeting in order to make the experience as efficient as possible.

Feel free to send additional suggestions to support@btcbytme.com, such that they could then find their way onto this list.
