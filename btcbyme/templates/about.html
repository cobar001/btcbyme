About

BtcByMe was created with the purpose of providing bitcoin users with a means to exchange fiat currencies for bitcoin in a private, personal, and offline manner. The site purposefully collects as little information as possible to provide a meaningful platform for users to connect and coordinate in-person meetings to make transactions. All transaction details are left for the users to agree upon, including time and location of the meeting, means by which the fiat and bitcoin may be exchanged (cash, wire transfer, on-chain transaction, LN transaction, etc.), and how to handle potential disputes.

Most bitcoin-fiat trades these days occur online through a variety of different services, most of which require you to provide personal information that is then stored on their servers (not to mention the recording of each and all of the different transactions one makes through their wallets, etc.). Though their services are quite convenient and have their use-cases, there should also be additional options that make acquiring bitcoin private, discrete, and undocumented by anything besides the bitcoin blockchain itself.

BtcByMe is currently a very small and relatively new operation based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States. Any feedback and financial donations are very welcome.

All Bitcoin prices are supplied via CoinGecko.
